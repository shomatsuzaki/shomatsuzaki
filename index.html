Hi! I'm an NYC-based Creative Director (Art Director or hybrid) with over a decade of experience working on brands such as IKEA, Bacardí, Macy’s, and Columbia Records. I studied computer science and theater at Yale University and previously worked as an interactive producer, where I developed a love for editing, animating, and prototyping. When not making ads, I’m either dancing hip-hop or dreaming of opening a bakery.

IKEA
It's the Little Things
IKEA's vision is to create a better everyday life for the many people, and that starts with creating products that everyone can afford. To highlight the brand's affordability, we created a spot that shows how IKEA really is for piggy banks of all sizes.
IKEA
Slice of Life
IKEA's first animated work in the U.S. was a Ghibli-inspired anime series we made for social during the back-to-college shopping season. The shorts were fully shoppable and were accompanied by animated GIF stickers, phone backgrounds, and a limited-edition manga available in print and online.
