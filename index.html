about sho

Sho is an NYC-based Art Director working at BBDO, where he specializes in both film and digital work. He graduated from Yale University, magna cum laude, where he studied Computer Science and Theatre. In addition to the typical Art Director role, Sho enjoys prototyping web-based projects and video editing/animation. Prior to being a Creative, he worked as a Senior Producer.
