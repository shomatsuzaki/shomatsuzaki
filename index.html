Hi! I'm a Creative Director (Art Director or hybrid) based in New York with over a decade of experience working on brands such as IKEA, Bacardí, Macy’s, and Columbia Records. I studied computer science and theater at Yale University and previously worked as an interactive producer, where I developed a love for editing, animating, and prototyping. When not making ads, I’m either dancing hip-hop or dreaming of opening a bakery.
If you'd like to chat, please email me at sho.g.matsuzaki@gmail.com or add me on LinkedIn here.
