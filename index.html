Hi! I'm an NYC-based Creative Director (Art Director or hybrid) with over a decade of experience working on brands such as IKEA, Bacardí, Macy’s, and Columbia Records. I studied computer science and theater at Yale University and previously worked as an interactive producer, where I developed a love for editing, animating, and prototyping. When not making ads, I’m either dancing hip-hop or dreaming of opening a bakery.
