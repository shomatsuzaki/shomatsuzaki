bacardi beat machine

To launch Bacardi's latest song and music video featuring Major Lazer and Anitta, we decided to hack YouTube and turn it into a DJ platform on which you could make your own remix of the song. The Beat Machine takes advantage of the underused keyboard "fast forward" feature on YouTube and allows you to trigger different parts of the song and music video like a soundboard.

The digital hack has been awarded two Webby's, the Grand Prix for the YouTube Works Awards, two ADC Silvers, and the FWA Site of the Day.
